Refund Policy

Introduction

This refund policy aims to clarify the conditions under which GemSync accepts refund requests for products or services purchased on our website gemsync.xyz.

1  -  Refund Conditions

Refund requests are accepted under the following circumstances:

- Order Error: If an order is placed by mistake, a refund can be requested within 24 hours of order confirmation.
- Technical Issue: If the purchased services experience malfunctions during the first month, a refund can be requested.
- Non-Compliance with Conditions: If the provided services do not match the descriptions or expectations specified at the time of purchase, a refund may be considered.

2  -  Refund Request Procedure

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

- Contact Support: Send a refund request to our customer support at support@gemsync.xyz. Please include your order number and a detailed description of the issue encountered.
- Request Validation: Our team will review your request and inform you of its approval or rejection within 5 business days.
- Refund: If the request is approved, the refund will be made using the same payment method used for the purchase within 14 days.

3  -  Refund Fees

GemSync does not charge fees for approved refunds. However, the original transaction fees will not be refunded.

4  -  Contact

For any questions regarding the refund policy or to submit a request, please contact our customer support at support@gemsync.xyz.
GemSync reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will take effect upon their publication on gemsync.xyz.
